Self Confidence

Sun 17Apr16

Relationship between Mindfulness and Self Awareness

They are one and the same. Self-awareness is being able to analyze your emotions without judgement. When you are aware of what is going on within you will react better to turbulent emotions. Attention is what drives neurological change.

Self Awareness Practice

I tried the body-scan. I found the scanning part of the exercise was pleasent and relaxing. I found it difficult to focus on a happy time for 3 minutes.

My Emotions are not me

We are not our emotions. We feel emotions but we don't have to let them dictate how we act.
