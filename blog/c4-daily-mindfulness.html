Daily Mindfulness Blog

Sun 10Apr16

'mindfulness conversation'

I learnt that it is harder to be silent then I thought. I have the propensity to jump in with my own thoughts on subjects and/or to finish sentences with what I think is the appropriate word.

My conversational partner appreciated being actively listened to.

suggested techniques to sustain the habit

To see meditation as a game is a good trick. Set the level on easy to begin with so that winning is not a struggle and to ensure a win comes every day. Then increase the difficulty/time. If I can take 1 mindful breath a day then I have done what I set out to do and anything more than that is a bonus.

Musings

It is heartening to read the walking baby analogy. Though it feels like we are making very little progress, in fact we are laying the ground work for a big leap forward. I look forward to that day.
